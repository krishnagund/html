    About US

    Our Inspiration

    Former minister, the Pioneer of Socio-economic transformation Late Shankarraoji Kolhe Saheb established Sanjivani Rural Education Society, Kopargaon in the year 1983 and started Sanjivani K. B. P. Polytechnic in the year 1982 and Sanjivani College of Engineering in the year 1983 with the view to impart technical education to the rural masses. The miracle took place many wards of farmers, workers, common people became the engineers and became self-reliant. With passage of time, to cope up with the demand of the people, he started pharmacy, MBA, and many more institutes having the world-class infrastructure and standards. 40 years back, he dreamed the vision to inculcate the skills and talents in the rural students so that they can face any kind of challenge across which they will come in the globe. And, it came really in existence that thousands of students from rural area got placed in multinational companies, some became government officers, some become entrepreneurs and some are shouldering the higher positions across the globe. Many students are going to pursue their MS in the leading universities of foreign countries and some are going for internship, that too on scholarship.

    Chairman's Desk

    Hon’ble Shri Nitindada Shankarrao Kolhe

    Sanjivani Rural Educational Society was established in 1983 with an aim to impart technical education to the doorsteps of rural society, as there was a dearth of technical manpower. In the last two decades College of Engineering and Polytechnic have achieved excellence in providing quality technical education. Students from all over India are seeking admission to these colleges. The College of Engineering is recognized by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi, permanently affiliated to Pune University and has been awarded Grade 'A' by the Government of Maharashtra. We are striving hard to provide all kinds of facilities in the campus to the students for all-round development. I have persuaded faculty members to acquire higher qualification from reputed Institutes and to do research related to the need of the Society.

    Director's Desk

    Director's Desk

    Dr.A.G.Thakur

    In an era of fast changing scientific and technological advancements, the degree of obsolescence is too high. This imposes very stringent requirements in the functioning of technical institutions which requires continuous upgradation and addition of facilities. Keeping this view in mind, our Institute has always been trying to provide the best of facilities possible for the students. We do expect our students to be extra ordinary in the present technological scenario of globalization. We do not expect our students to be confined only to their academic studies, rather encourage them to take an active part in co-curricular and extra curricular activities also. For this, various platforms are made available like departmental associations, National and International students associations, etc. The mission of this Institute is to develop the Technical Human Resources towards socio-economic growth of Rural India for global competitiveness. I am quite sure that with the earnest co-operation of our students, their guardians and the faculty, we shall be able to achieve the same.
